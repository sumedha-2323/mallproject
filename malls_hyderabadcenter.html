Hyderabad Center Mall:


Hyderabad Central Mall is a shopping mall in Hyderabad, India, located on the Punjagutta Road. The mall includes national and international brands, as well as a range of movies, music, books, coffee shops, a food court, supermarket and restaurants. At the Hyderabad Central Mall, shopping is spread over five levels.

The Hyderabad Central Mall was launched in November 2004 when the owners, Pantaloon Retail (India) Limited, at the same time also announced plans to created 12 to 15 malls across India within two years. Hyderabad Central Mall has been built with an investment of some Rs.70 crore.

Well structured, organized, planned well, spacious, ample space to sit and walk through. All the products, you name it and we get them there – that is Hyderabad Central Mall, which revolutionized the mall culture in Hyderabad. World class standards in ambience, interiors and service, the Hyderabad Central Mall has been conceptualised with a theme and tagline that says “Shop, Eat and Celebrate.”

The sprawling mall spread over 2,50,000 square feet has everything a family needs. Hyderabad Central Mall has a separate section for services such as travel, finance, investment, insurance, cinema ticket booking, bill payments, foreign exchange and other miscellaneous services. It also has a “Central Square”, a place for product launches, impromptu events, displays, shows and exhibitions.

First floor of Hyderabad Central Mall is devoted for food and women, second floor for men, third floor for youth and fourth floor for kids and home accessories, including a food court. Fifth floor of Hyderabad Central Mall has a 5 screen multiplex by famous PVR Cinemas with a seating capacity of 1,322 persons.

Talking about the food, the Hyderabad Central Mall has seven restaurants, Fuel Station, Pizza Hut, Subway and Java Green are one of them. The mall also has an ice cream parlour and Noodle Bar restaurant.

Hyderabad Central Mall is able to accommodate 300 cars and equal number of two-wheelers at a time. Valet parking is also available.
