Manjeera Trinity Mall:


Manjeera Trinity Mall is one of the recently opened malls in the Hyderabad city, which slowly and steadily converting into a marvelous mall city. So, we at CityHyd visited this place several times and figured out that this mall certainly have a good layout and facilities, at least at this moment to be recommended by us as one of the fine places to be in Hyderabad.

This mall is located at KPHB road, just opposite to the JNTU main gate. This is one mall which of world class standards that has been opened in the Kukatpally area. Obviously, a big relief for the people living in and around that area who usually had to travel to other place those were 5+ kilometers away. The mall is obviously still not fully functional because the shops are still either opening or waiting to be leased, but whatever has been opened seems to be nice and classy.


Big Pluses For Manjeera Trinity Mall

The location of the mall is on the main road, which is not always as crowded as one would see, so it’s an okay location. The mall occupies a huge space, which makes it really cool. The parking is huge and well planned. The elevators and escalators are also high-tech and in good number so it’s easy to move inside the mall from one floor to another. Additional pluses of Manjeera Trinity Mall are:


  • Cineplex: Cinepolis Movie Cinemas
  • Hyper City Store- India’s Walmart!
  • Branded Shopping Stores- Addidas, Reebok, Jewelers, International Merchandise etc.
  • Food Court on top floor- KFC, Pizzas, Coffee, Shakes, Meals etc.
  • McDonald on ground floor

Address
